Talent Access Platform — Finish Phase

Product charter (locked)



This product is a controlled access platform to pre-qualified, interest-verified candidates.



It is not a job board.

It is not an ATS.

It is not an interview system.



Core flow:



Company → Job



Job → curated tank



Client → views candidates



Client → requests intros



Admin → manages intros



The platform serves a membership business.



Out of scope:



applicant tracking



interview scheduling



messaging systems



workflow stages



ATS features
